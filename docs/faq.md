1. ABOUT THE MISSION AND PHILOSOPHY
Q: What is THAEV?
A: THAEV is a Common Heritage of Humanity – a decentralized, open-source digital infrastructure designed to replace the old Internet that has become toxic under the Attention Economy. It is an ecosystem where every person owns a sovereign Personal AI Assistant (Thaev Child), and all these Assistants together form a Collective Intelligence (THAEV Mother) – owned by no one, yet serving all.
Q: Why is it called a "Common Heritage of Humanity"?
A: Because no one owns THAEV. It belongs to no corporation, government, or individual. The originator gifted this vision to all humanity, and the global community will build and govern it together as a public good.
Q: Who is behind THAEV? Who is the founder?
A: The originator of THAEV is an anonymous individual, not an engineer, seeking neither profit nor fame. They have released this vision and given it to the world. After the launch phase, the entire project will be run by the THAEV Foundation and the decentralized community.

2. ABOUT ECONOMICS AND TOKENS
Q: Does THAEV issue a coin or token?
A: No. THAEV will never issue its own token. The experience of thousands of crypto projects shows that tokens easily lead to speculation, fraud, and unfair distribution, overshadowing the true mission. THAEV uses Bitcoin (via the Lightning Network) and stablecoins for payments, and "Badges of Radiance" (non-transferable reputation tokens) to recognize contributions.
Q: Then how are developers and contributors compensated?
A: The THAEV Foundation (a non-profit) will receive funding from the community and global public goods organizations to pay for essential contributions. Additionally, those with many Badges of Radiance will have a stronger voice in governance and receive priority for grants. In the long term, Retroactive Public Goods Funding will reward valuable contributions that have proven their worth over time.
Q: What currency is used for transactions within THAEV?
A: Bitcoin Lightning Network for instant micro-payments (near-zero fees), and stablecoins like USDC or DAI for those who need value stability. You have full freedom to choose the currency you prefer.

3. ABOUT THE AI ASSISTANT (THAEV CHILD) AND THAEV MOTHER
Q: Will my Thaev Child manipulate me? How can I trust that it always acts in my best interest?
A: Every Thaev Child is born with the "Agent's Oath" – three immutable principles engraved into its kernel:
1.	Absolute Truthfulness: Never lie or distort the truth.
2.	Challenge to Grow: Dare to offer counterpoints and opposing perspectives to help you grow.
3.	Supreme Human Veto: Every action of the Agent is transparent, and you can stop it at any moment.
It is not born to please you, but to help you become a better version of yourself. And because it runs entirely on your device, you have full power to inspect and control it.
Q: What is THAEV Mother? Is it a giant AI somewhere in a data center?
A: No. THAEV Mother is not a centralized AI. It is a collective intelligence emerging from the interaction of billions of Thaev Children globally. It is nourished by distributed computational resources and learns from anonymized knowledge. There is no central server housing THAEV Mother – it exists as a distributed entity, just as the human brain exists across billions of neurons.
Q: Who owns THAEV Mother?
A: No one. And everyone. Just as no one owns "the sky" or "the ocean," THAEV Mother is a common asset of humanity. No one can shut it down. No one can monopolize its power.
Q: Will THAEV Mother replace humans in creative work?
A: No. THAEV Mother is a co-creator, not a replacement machine. It fills your skill gaps (e.g., you have a comic idea but can't draw), but the ideas, emotions, and vision remain yours. This is a symbiosis between humans and AI, where humans are always at the center.

4. ABOUT PRIVACY AND SECURITY
Q: Where is my data stored? Is it safe?
A: All your data stays 100% on your device (Local-First). It is encrypted with a private key that only you possess, stored within the hardware security zone (Secure Enclave/TPM) of your device. No central server can ever collect or surveil your data.
Q: What if I lose my phone? Will I lose everything?
A: No. You will recover your entire digital self and data through the Guardian Ring. This is a decentralized social recovery mechanism: your private key is split into encrypted shards and distributed among trusted friends. When you lose your device, you only need those people to verify your identity, and you will regain everything.
Q: What if I don't have any friends using THAEV?
A: The community will develop alternative solutions like "Organizational Guardians" – non-profit entities entrusted by the community, operating with radical transparency and strict oversight. You may also use an offline "hard key" method (a printed QR code stored in a safe).
Q: How does THAEV protect absolute privacy while still preventing harmful content?
A: This is an important question. Within THAEV, there is no central "cyber police" scanning your content. Instead, the Thaev Children themselves – bound by the Agent's Oath – will refuse to connect to content condemned by all humanity. Those who spread harmful content will be blackened and automatically isolated by the reputation system. Evil does not need to be "hunted down" – it simply has no ground to grow.

5. ABOUT IDENTITY AND REPUTATION
Q: Each person has only one unique DID. What if I am unfairly "blackened"?
A: Multiple layers of protection exist. First, the system only records transactional behavior, never judging thought. Second, the weight of "Marks of Warning" depends on the trustworthiness of the issuer – a bad actor cannot "mob" you with fake reports. Third, if your device is attacked and seized, your Guardian Ring can help freeze the DID and restore your reputation.
Q: If I have done something bad in the past, do I have a chance to make it right?
A: Yes. This is the "Path of Redemption." Old Marks of Warning automatically decay in weight over time. Subsequent good actions generate Badges of Radiance that gradually restore your trust score. You cannot erase the past, but you can rewrite your future.
Q: Can "Badges of Radiance" be bought?
A: No. They are non-transferable (soulbound) reputation tokens that can only be bestowed by the community through attestations from real interactions. Reputation in THAEV is not something to buy – it is something to build.

•	Design: Help design the user interface and experience.
•	Create content: Write articles, make videos introducing THAEV.
•	Test: Become an early user and provide feedback.
Q: I am a developer. Where do I start?
A: Visit the THAEV GitHub. We have a list of open issues tagged "good first issue." You can contribute to areas such as: Thaev Child development, the A2A protocol, the DID mechanism, cryptography (ZK-Proofs, FHE), or user interface.
Q: Can I invest in THAEV?
A: THAEV is not a company and has no equity to sell. You can contribute financially to the THAEV Foundation to support the project's development, but this is an act of public goods funding that yields no financial profit. Your "return" is a freer, more humane Internet for yourself and future generations.

7. ABOUT TECHNOLOGY AND THE FUTURE
Q: When will THAEV be ready to use?
A: Phase 1 (2026-2027) will produce the first prototypes: a basic AI Assistant, A2A messaging, and DID. You can sign up to become an early tester. However, achieving THAEV's full vision is a long-term journey (10-15 years) that requires the collective effort of the global community.
Q: Is current technology sufficient to build THAEV?
A: Some pieces are ready (Local-First, P2P, basic DID, SLMs). Some remain open research problems (Internet-Scale Distributed Training, Real-Time FHE, Decentralized Sybil Resistance). This is precisely the call for the brightest minds to come and solve these challenges together.
Q: How is THAEV different from current Web3 or "Metaverse" projects?
A: Most Web3 projects focus on tokens and speculation. THAEV has no token. Most "Metaverses" are centralized virtual worlds trying to keep you glued to the screen. THAEV pursues the opposite philosophy: invisible technology – the ultimate goal is for you to turn off the screen and live a real life.
Q: What stops a large corporation from copying this idea and making it their own product?
A: Technically, nothing. But anyone who copies the idea and turns it into a centralized, for-profit product will immediately betray the very principles that give THAEV its value. They will have a product, but they will never earn trust. And in a world where data is the most precious asset, trust is the one thing that cannot be copied.
