PREAMBLE
This document is a formal legal declaration, establishing the irrevocable nature of THAEV as a Common Heritage of Humanity. It defines the terms under which all intellectual property of THAEV — including text, source code, designs, and all related materials — is dedicated in perpetuity to the global public.

ARTICLE 1: COMMON HERITAGE DECLARATION
1.1. Legal Status
THAEV, including the Manifesto, the Whitepaper, all technical documentation, source code, protocols, and every other constituent component, is dedicated in perpetuity as a Common Heritage of Humanity.
1.2. Irrevocability
This dedication is permanent and irrevocable. Once published, no individual, corporation, or nation — including the Originator — shall have the right to revoke, privatize, or impose new restrictions on these assets without violating the spirit of the Common Heritage.
1.3. No Centralized Ownership
No single entity owns THAEV. It belongs to all humanity, managed and developed by the global community through the THAEV Foundation and decentralized governance mechanisms.

ARTICLE 4: PROTECTION OF THE HERITAGE'S INTEGRITY
4.1. Against Privatization
THAEV is designed to resist any attempt at privatization. Strong Copyleft licenses (CC BY-SA 4.0 and GPLv3) ensure that all derivative versions must retain the same open spirit, creating a "legal fence" that prevents the transformation of the Common Heritage into proprietary property.
4.2. Forking as a Fundamental Right
If any entity attempts to control or distort the mission of THAEV, the community has the right to "fork" — to branch off and continue development based on the same source code and documentation, under the same licenses. The right to fork is the ultimate shield protecting the Common Heritage.

The name "THAEV" and the slogan "FOR HUMANITY / VÌ CON NGƯỜI" are protected as part of the Common Heritage. Any derivative product using this name must comply with the Manifesto and the core principles of THAEV. The THAEV Foundation, as the representative entrusted by the community, has the responsibility to protect the integrity of the name and brand.
