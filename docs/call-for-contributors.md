TO THE BUILDERS OF THE FUTURE
Dear Friend,
What you hold in your hands is not just an invitation. It is a call to action.
We are building THAEV — a Common Heritage of Humanity, a new Internet where technology becomes invisible and human beings are set free. This is not the project of a company, a corporation, or an individual. This is the project of all of us.
And to turn this grand vision into reality, we need you.
Whoever you are — a seasoned developer, a cryptography researcher, a designer passionate about minimalism, an artist yearning for creative freedom, or simply a human being who wants the world to be better — you have a place in THAEV.
________________________________________
WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR
1. Software Developers & Engineers
You write the backbone of THAEV. We need:
•	Rust, Go, C/C++ Developers: To build the high-performance core components: the A2A protocol, the P2P network based on libp2p, and lightweight consensus mechanisms.
•	Mobile Developers (Swift, Kotlin, React Native/Flutter): To develop the Thaev Child application on iOS and Android, where users meet their AI Assistant.
•	On-Device AI/ML Engineers: Those with experience in small language models (SLMs), on-device fine-tuning (LoRA, QLoRA), and optimizing inference on mobile NPUs/GPUs.
•	Frontend Developers: To craft the "Void" (Zero-UI) experience — an interface that is empty yet contains invisible power.
2. Cryptographers & Security Researchers
You are the guardians of Digital Sovereignty for billions of people. We need:
•	Zero-Knowledge Proofs Specialists: To implement zk-SNARKs and zk-STARKs for "blind interviews," compatibility proofs, and transaction verification.
•	Homomorphic Encryption (FHE/SMPC) Specialists: To realize blind computations on encrypted data.
•	Post-Quantum Cryptography Specialists: To ensure THAEV endures against all future threats.
•	Security Auditors: To review source code, find vulnerabilities, and protect the ecosystem.
3. AI & Machine Learning Researchers
You light the fire for THAEV Mother. We need:
•	Federated Learning & Distributed Learning Specialists: To research and develop algorithms that allow billions of devices to learn together without centralizing data.
•	Continual Learning Specialists: To solve the problem of "learning without forgetting" for the Agents.
•	Natural Language Processing (NLP) Specialists: To improve the understanding and companionship capabilities of the Thaev Child.
•	Multimodal Generation Specialists: To build the bridge between human ideas and creative works (text, image, audio, video).
4. Designers & Experience Architects
You make technology invisible. We need:
•	UX/UI Designers for Zero-UI: To define a new design language where "nothing" is "everything." The user experience must be as natural as breathing.
•	Graphic & Motion Designers: To create visuals, icons, and animations for the THAEV ecosystem.
•	Voice UI Specialists: To design natural, emotionally rich conversations between humans and the Thaev Child.
•	Accessibility Specialists: To ensure THAEV is for everyone, regardless of ability.
5. Content Creators & Communicators
You tell the story of THAEV to the world. We need:
•	Writers & Editors: To write blog posts, documentation, and inspiring stories.
•	Translators: To translate the Manifesto and all materials into every language on Earth. THAEV must speak your tongue.
•	Video & Podcast Creators: To explain THAEV in an accessible and engaging way for all audiences.
•	Community Managers: To nurture and coordinate the community on Discord, GitHub, and social platforms.
6. Legal & Governance Specialists
You protect THAEV from the traps of the old world. We need:
•	Intellectual Property & Open Source Lawyers: To advise on licensing and protect the Common Heritage from privatization.
•	Non-Profit & Decentralized Governance Specialists: To establish and operate the THAEV Foundation and community voting mechanisms.
•	Privacy & Data Protection Specialists (GDPR, etc.): To ensure THAEV complies with all global regulations without betraying its philosophy.
7. Pioneer Users & Testers
You are the first to step into THAEV and help shape it. We need:
•	Beta Testers: To install alpha/beta builds, use them daily, and report bugs and feedback.
•	People with Special Needs: To help us ensure THAEV is truly for everyone.
•	People from Every Culture: So that THAEV can understand the diversity of humanity.

HOW TO GET STARTED
Step 1: Read the Manifesto and Whitepaper
Begin by understanding the vision. Read the THAEV Manifesto (available in Vietnamese, English, and Chinese) and the Technical Whitepaper at thaev.com.
Step 2: Join the Community
•	Discord: [Discord link] — The place for lively discussion, asking questions, and connecting with like-minded people.
•	GitHub: https://github.com/thaev — Where all source code and open issues reside.
Step 3: Pick Your First Task
•	On GitHub, look for issues tagged "good first issue" or "help wanted".
•	On Discord, introduce yourself in the #introductions channel and let us know how you want to contribute.
Step 4: Submit Your First Contribution
•	Fork the repository, create a branch, and send your first Pull Request. Don't worry if it's not perfect — our community is always ready to help and guide you.

•	Grant Opportunities: The THAEV Foundation will allocate grants for significant contributions, based on proven reputation and merit.
