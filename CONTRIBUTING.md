# Contributing to THAEV

Thank you for being here. THAEV is a Common Heritage of Humanity — it belongs to all of us, and your contribution matters.

## 🌱 Getting Started

1. **Read the [Manifesto](docs/manifesto.md)** to feel the vision.
2. **Browse the [FAQ](docs/faq.md)** for quick answers.
3. **Pick a task** — see below.

## 🛠️ Ways to Contribute

| What | Where |
|---|---|
| **Code** (AI, P2P, Crypto, UI) | `thaev-child/`, `a2a-protocol/`, `did/`, `ui/` |
| **Documentation** (fixes, translations) | `docs/` |
| **Design** (logos, mockups) | `assets/` |
| **Ideas & Feedback** | Open an [Issue](https://github.com/thaev/thaev/issues) |

## 📋 Your First Contribution

1. Check the **"good first issue"** label in the [Issues](https://github.com/thaev/thaev/issues) tab.
2. Fork the repository and create a branch.
3. Make your changes and open a Pull Request.
4. A maintainer will review it. Don't worry if it's not perfect — we learn together.

## 🏅 Recognition

Meaningful contributions earn a **Badge of Radiance** — a permanent, non-transferable reputation token anchored to your DID. This badge gives you a voice in THAEV's future governance.

## 🤝 Community

- Be kind and respectful.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

We are building a new Internet for humanity. Let's build it together.

**FOR HUMANITY.**
